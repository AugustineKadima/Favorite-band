Sauti Sol
Africa's best band

My most favorite band in Kenya are Sauti Sol. I have been their number one fan over the years simply because they represent the Kenyan culture in their decent music.

They sing afro-pop music and were formed in Nairobi, Kenya by vocalists Bien-Aim√© Baraza , Willis Chimano and Savara Mudigi in 2005.

They released their debut studio album, Mwanzo , on 1st November, 2008. Their second studio album, Sol Filosofia , was released on 25 February 2011, earning them a number of accolades and nominations in the process.

On 18th June, 2012, they released a self-titled extended play in collaboration with South African rapper and record producer Spoek Mathambo.

Their third studio album, Live and Die in Afrika , was released online on 21 November 2015 as a free download, available for 48 hours as an early Christmas present.

They have had several successful tours in Africa and Europe, topped Kenyan charts and gained international attention with shows in Europe and the US, as well as television appearances and various accolades and nominations, including the 2011 concert performance in Kenya with South African acappella group, Ladysmith Black Mambazo.

They have also received both local and international accolades and nominations, including at the Kisima Music Awards, the Channel O Music Video Awards, the MTV Europe Music Awards, and the BET Awards.
